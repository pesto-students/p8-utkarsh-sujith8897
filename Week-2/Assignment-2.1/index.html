The Benefits of Plant-Based Eating for Your Health and the Environment

Plant-based diets, which are rich in fruits, vegetables, legumes, and whole grains, have been shown to have numerous health and environmental benefits. For starters, plant-based eating has been linked to a lower risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers. This is likely due to the fact that plant-based diets are typically high in fiber, antioxidants, and other nutrients that are important for good health.
